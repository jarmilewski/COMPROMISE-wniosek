The project requires access to rare research equipment and expertise. No participants hold all the necessary instrumentation and expertise to do the project alone. The two nations have different abilities to contribute to the project. Namely: The WUT (Warsaw University of Technology) has expertise in Molten Carbonate Fuel Cell testing and manufacturing as well as characterizing the porous layers by electrochemical MCFC testing and Raman spectroscopy and the XRD technique.
