The NCU (National Central University) has experience in the preparation of proton conducting solid oxide fuel cells consisting of a proton conductor via tape casting, co-sintering and sample characterization by means of TGA, DSC, Raman, FIB, HRTEM, GC-Mass, CV, and EIS techniques. An exchange of partners and experiences will produce deeper cooperation, more complementary sample characterization and more effective fuel cell construction. The cooperation between WUT and NCU groups will be very intense during the proposed project. This project will be the first stage of a fruitful and permanent cooperation.
